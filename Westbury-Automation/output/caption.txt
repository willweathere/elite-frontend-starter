Sometimes the problem is not that a customer will not pay. It is that you cannot find them. Businesses move, close, or change hands, and the invoice goes unanswered.

This is where tracing helps. We locate people and businesses that have become hard to reach, so the debt can be dealt with properly rather than written off.

It is one of several services we offer alongside collection, litigation and enforcement. If a debtor has gone quiet and you are not sure where to turn next, learn more on our website.
