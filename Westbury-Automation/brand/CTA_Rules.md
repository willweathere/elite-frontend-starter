# Call To Action Rules

Not every post needs a CTA.

Approved CTAs (use at most one, and only if it fits naturally):
- Learn more on our website.
- Get in touch today.
- Speak with our team.
- Contact us to discuss your situation.
- Read our latest insight.

Rules:
- Never sound pushy.
- Never pressure the reader.
- The CTA should feel natural and low-friction.
