# Image Rules

## Sizes
- **LinkedIn / Instagram portrait:** 1080 × 1350 (default for this system)
- **Instagram square:** 1080 × 1080
- **X/Twitter landscape:** 1200 × 675

## Every image must
- Use Westbury branding and the correct colours (see Design_Rules.md).
- Include the logo (wordmark lockup bottom-right, or the Logo.png badge).
- Match the topic of the caption.
- Look like a professional, considered LinkedIn post from a real agency.
- Have strong visual hierarchy and clear, readable text.

## Every image must avoid
- Generic "AI-generated" looks — no warped text, no fake logos, no random shapes.
- Overcrowded layouts, clip-art, drop shadows, 3D renders.
- Colours outside the brand palette.
- Anything that looks intimidating or threatening.

## Text on the image
- Eyebrow: 2–4 words, letterspaced caps.
- Headline: 2–4 short lines, sentence case, ends with a full stop.
- Subline: one calm, reassuring line.
- Keep total on-image text short — the caption carries the detail.
