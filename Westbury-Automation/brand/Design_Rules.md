# Design Rules

The visual language every Westbury image must follow. Extracted from the brand's own posts
and logo.

## Colours
| Role | Name | Hex |
|---|---|---|
| Primary field | Westbury Crimson | `#C8102E` |
| Depth (gradient top) | Deep Maroon | `#3F0811` |
| Seasonal dark | Plum Noir | `#1A0910` |
| Accent | Champagne Gold | `#DEB26E` |
| Watermark | Blush Pink | `#F2A8B5` |
| Text | White | `#FFFFFF` |

- Background is **always** a crimson gradient (Deep Maroon → Crimson), or Plum Noir for
  seasonal dark posts. Never flat white, never off-palette colours.
- Gold is used only at small scale (eyebrow labels, sublines, hairline rules).
- A fine film grain sits over every background so posts feel printed, not digital.

## Typography (three voices, in this order)
1. **Eyebrow** — letterspaced serif caps, gold, small. (Crimson Pro)
2. **Headline** — high-contrast Didone serif, white, large, sentence case, ends with a full
   stop, max 4 lines. (Gloock / Playfair Display family)
3. **Subline** — warm italic serif, one reassuring line. (Crimson Pro Italic)

## Layout
- Margins ~8.5% of the shorter edge. Nothing crosses them except the watermark.
- Low centre of gravity: keep the top open, the type block sits around 54% height.
- **Archetype A (left-aligned)** for educational/tips. **Archetype B (centered, with a gold
  diamond divider)** for credibility/greetings.
- Footer: italic sign-off bottom-left, logo lockup bottom-right.
- The oversized "W" watermark (Blush Pink, ~30% opacity) bleeds off the right edge.

## Feel
Premium, calm, professional, trustworthy. Understated — never busy, never aggressive.
