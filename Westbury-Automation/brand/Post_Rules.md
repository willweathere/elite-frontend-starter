# Post Rules

Every post must:
- Be original. Never copy competitors.
- Educate, inform or build trust.
- Provide genuinely useful information.
- Read as though written by a real member of the marketing team.

Never:
- Sound like AI.
- Use emojis.
- Use hashtags (unless specifically asked).
- Overuse exclamation marks.
- Use corporate buzzwords or clickbait.
- Make legal claims.
- Make promises that cannot be proven.
- Invent statistics.
- Mention prices.
- Mention competitors.
- Repeat a previous post.

Always:
- Write naturally, in UK English.
- Keep sentences and paragraphs short.
- Keep the reading level simple.
- Use true facts only (see Brand_Info.md).
- Do not overuse bold text.

**Length:** around 80–150 words unless instructed otherwise.
