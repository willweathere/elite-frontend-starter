# Westbury Collections — Social Media Content Strategy

## Posting frequency

- **Instagram:** 4–5 feed posts per week (default cadence: Mon, Tue, Thu, Fri, + Sat when a
  campaign is running). Stories can mirror each feed post same-day.
- **X/Twitter:** repurpose 3 of the week's posts at 1200×675.
- One post per day maximum — Westbury's voice is unhurried; scarcity reads as premium.

## Best posting times (UK audience)

| Slot | Time (UK) | Best for |
|---|---|---|
| Midday | 12:00–13:00 | Tips, educational, showroom |
| Evening | 18:30–20:00 | Inspiration, storytelling, seasonal |
| Weekend morning | Sat 09:30–11:00 | Campaigns, community, greetings |

Default queue times in Buffer: **12:15 and 19:00 weekdays, 10:00 Saturday.** Review Buffer/IG
analytics monthly and shift slots toward whatever the data shows.

## Weekly content structure

| Day | Category | Mood |
|---|---|---|
| Monday | Design tip / educational | Editorial, left-aligned layout |
| Tuesday | Product or collection showcase | Photography-led |
| Wednesday | *(rest day — Stories only)* | — |
| Thursday | Interior inspiration / completed project | Photography-led |
| Friday | Brand storytelling / behind the scenes / team | Centered formal layout |
| Saturday | Showroom / community / customer story | Warm, CTA to visit |
| Sunday | *(rest, or seasonal greeting when the calendar calls for it)* | — |

## Category rotation

Rotate through all ten categories; no category twice in one week except during a named
seasonal campaign:

1. Product showcases
2. Luxury interior inspiration
3. Design tips
4. Completed projects
5. Customer stories
6. Showroom content
7. Behind the scenes
8. Seasonal campaigns
9. Educational posts
10. Brand storytelling

**Seasonal campaigns** (Halloween, Christmas, spring refresh, etc.) may take over the grid for
their week, using the dark Plum Noir variant where the occasion suits.

## Guardrails

- Customer stories and completed projects require the customer's written permission and the
  real details from the brief — nothing invented.
- Promotions appear only when supplied by the business, with exact wording and dates.
- Every post ships with alt text.
