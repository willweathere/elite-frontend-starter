# Westbury Collections — Social Media Content Strategy

Westbury Collections Ltd is a UK commercial debt recovery firm. Content exists to **educate,
inform and build trust** — never to pressure. The primary platform for this B2B audience is
**LinkedIn**, with Instagram and X as supporting channels.

## Audience

Business owners, finance directors, credit controllers, and sectors that live with late
payment — construction, schools, trades, SMEs. People who are often stressed about unpaid
invoices and cashflow. Speak to them calmly and helpfully.

## Posting frequency

- **LinkedIn (primary):** 3–4 posts per week.
- **Instagram:** 3–4 feed posts per week (mirror the LinkedIn topics, portrait format).
- **X/Twitter:** repurpose 2–3 of the week's posts at 1200×675.
- One post per day maximum. Quiet consistency reads as credible.

## Best posting times (UK, B2B audience)

| Slot | Time (UK) | Best for |
|---|---|---|
| Early | 08:00–09:00 | Educational / insight (caught before the working day) |
| Midday | 12:00–13:00 | Tips, service explainers |
| Afternoon | 16:30–17:30 | Trust / credibility, lighter posts |

Avoid evenings and weekends for a B2B audience. Default Buffer queue: **08:30 and 12:30 on
Tue/Wed/Thu**, plus **12:30 Monday** when a fourth post runs. Review analytics monthly and adjust.

## Weekly content structure

| Day | Category | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Monday | Educational / how it works | Explain a part of the recovery process plainly |
| Tuesday | Cashflow & credit-control tips | Practical help business owners can use today |
| Wednesday | *(rest — or LinkedIn-only insight)* | — |
| Thursday | Service spotlight OR trust/credibility | One of the 8 services, or a true proof point |
| Friday | Approach / myth-busting / team | Show the calm, relationship-first personality |
| Weekend | *(rest, or a seasonal greeting when the calendar calls for it)* | — |

## Content categories (rotate — no category twice in a week)

1. **Educational** — how debt recovery actually works; the stages; what to expect.
2. **Cashflow & credit-control tips** — invoicing, terms, chasing politely, prevention.
3. **Service spotlights** — debt collection, litigation, asset searches, tracing, enforcement,
   adjudication, insolvency, process serving (one at a time, explained simply).
4. **Myth-busting** — e.g. "recovery doesn't have to damage the relationship".
5. **Trust & credibility** — real proof points only (£110M+ recovered, 3,500+ clients, 98%
   satisfaction, since 2019). Never invented.
6. **Sector insight** — late payment in construction, schools, trades, SMEs.
7. **Approach & values** — professional, ethical, friendly, transparent.
8. **Behind the scenes / team** — the 16-person team, how the firm works (with permission).
9. **FAQs** — common questions from business owners, answered plainly.
10. **Seasonal / brand moments** — greetings and calendar days, kept tasteful.

## Guardrails (non-negotiable — from the brand's post rules)

- No emojis. No hashtags unless specifically requested. UK English. 80–150 words.
- No legal claims, no unprovable promises, no invented statistics, no prices, no competitor names.
- Never aggressive, threatening or pushy — even subtly.
- Facts only from the brand brief. Anything involving a legal point, a client name, or a new
  claim goes to a human for approval before publishing.
- Every post must read as though written by a real member of the marketing team.
