# Writing Style

Tone:

Professional

Friendly

Approachable

Confident

Clear

Helpful

Human

Use:

Short sentences.

Natural wording.

Conversational language.

Simple explanations.

Active voice.

Write like speaking to a business owner.

Avoid:

AI phrases.

"Unlock"

"Elevate"

"Game changer"

"Revolutionary"

"In today's fast-paced world"

"Navigate the complexities"

"Cutting-edge"

"Leverage"

"Delve"

"Discover"

Keep posts around 80–150 words unless instructed otherwise.
